Frequently Asked Questions

What is a payment channel?

Within the context of Asyncpay, a payment channel is connection to a payment provider that allows businesses to receive money on the internet e.g Paystack, Stripe, Flutterwave. When you create a payment channel, Asyncpay can connect to a payment provider like Paystack through that payment channel. A payment channel is always tied to a payment provider.

What types of payment methods can I use on Asyncpay?

Asyncpay support card payments across multiple payment providers. We will be supporting lots of other payment methods like USSD and bank transfers by Q3 2023.

How many payment providers does Asyncpay support?

Asyncpay currently supports three payment providers: Paystack, Stripe and Flutterwave. We'll be providing support for a whole lot more in the coming months. We intend to have at least 20 connections by the end of Q3.

Are my customers' information secured?

Yes, Asyncpay takes a lot of measures to ensure your customer information is secure. We encrypt all sensitive data stored in our systems and adhere to all best practices for managing user data.

Do you offer customer support if I have questions or issues with using your system?

Definitely! Feel free to reach out to us at info@asyncpay.io and we'll respond immediately

Does Asyncpay provide webhooks?

Yes, we push webhooks for all events that happen in your system and we provide multiple ways of staying in the know, including sms and email. We also do the heavy work of getting the webhooks from all the payment channels you connect to and sending it all to you in one consistent format. You don't have to worry how any payment channel structures their data. Asyncpay has you covered.
